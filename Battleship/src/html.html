Battleship

Game rules

Your fleet is on the left. Your opponent is a computer.
Each fleet has 8 ships:

1 carrier (size 4), 2 battleships (size 3), 2 cruisers (size 2), and 3 destroyers (size 1).

Ships cannot "touch" each other, there's at least 1 square gap between them in all directions.

The first player is determined randomly. If it's the computer, the game starts immediately.
